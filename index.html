Welcome to the ελληνικά Language Page.

you probably noticed on the Phoenician page that the Greek characters also represented a number. the previously unmentioned ελληνικά symbol is ϡ (named "sampi" and represents 900). along with ϟ(koppa 90) ϛ(digamma 6), ϡ is just a number and not a ελληνικά letter.
